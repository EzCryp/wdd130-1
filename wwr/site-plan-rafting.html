Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Rafting Site is to provide information about the rafting sites and the services they offer. The site will include information about the company, the trips they offer, and how to contact them.

On a very basic level, whitewater rafting is taking a raft through whitewater. Whitewater is a form of river water that is characterized by fast, shallow stretches of water. These shallow stretches are known as 'rapids' and come in different 'grades' or levels of choppiness/difficulty of passage.

Branding

Audience

The target audience for the Rafting Site is people who are interested in outdoor activities and adventure. The site will be designed to appeal to people who enjoy the outdoors and are looking for a fun and exciting experience.
